FAQ

How do I use the tool?

Upload a Keyword Planner CSV or paste a list → click Build interactive list → click words or tick whole lines → To negatives → export CSV for Google Ads Editor or copy into the UI.

What does selecting a whole line do?

The row checkbox adds the full phrase as an exact-match negative [phrase]. Clicking a single word adds it as a broad negative that applies wherever that token appears.

“To negatives” vs “Keep list (whitelist)”?

To negatives collects everything you marked (words and lines). Keep list (whitelist) collects lines you did not touch — handy when only a few rows are irrelevant.

Can I import Keyword Planner CSV?

Yes — use the export as-is. Keywords are read automatically; extra columns are ignored.

How do I push results to Google Ads?

Use Export CSV for Google Ads Editor, or Copy and paste into the interface.
