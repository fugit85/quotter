FAQ

How do I remove duplicate lines?

Paste one item per line and click Deduplicate. Repeating lines are removed instantly.

Are “buy sofa” and “sofa buy” treated as duplicates?

Not by default. Enable Ignore word order (lines) to treat same words in any order as one line.

Does it work with large lists?

Yes — processing stays in the browser; nothing is uploaded. There is no line limit.

Can I deduplicate words instead of lines?

Yes — choose Unique words. The text is split into tokens and only unique words remain.

Is original line order kept?

Yes — the first occurrence in each duplicate group is kept; order of the remaining lines is unchanged.
